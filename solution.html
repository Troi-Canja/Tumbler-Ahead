Solving the Problems

1. Removable Bottom Cover




Providing a tiny space at the bottom lid of the tumbler allows the user to store little stuffs that suits the space provision. It might be too simple but it creates a big difference.

A tumbler with multiple functions is so superb and will be considered the next-gen trend. You don't have to struggle looking for your keys among the ocean of your clutters.



2. Device Tracker




It's such an anxiety-trigerring event when you forgot where you've put your device, right?

Worry no more since this smart tumbler is equipped with tracking feature which notifies the user if the user's registered device is out of the 3-meter radius. If the tumbler wasn't informed that your device is supposedly not inside the certain radius, it alarms and/or vibrate that will serve as a signal for you about your misplaced device. If your device's gps is on, you can track it through the tumbler's map. How handy could it be?

1. 2D Hologram







As modern community dive deeper to digitalized world, we gradually equip even the simplest thing with technology. Can you imagine the convenience it will give us if a tumbler, something that we usually have anywhere we go, be incorporated with technological innovations? Let us show you a brief summary of what we envision a smart tumbler.
Lens generating 2d-hologram that are motion sensitive allowing interactive activity with the user. It allows basic device activities such as:
• Setting up appointment and alarms
• Browsing various search engines
• Counts the liquid intake of the user
• Tracks the number of steps of the user has done for a day
• Bluetooth connectivity
• Voice recording
• Time, calendar and notes display
